អំពីពួកយើង

At IT Cafe, we're more than just a coffee shop – we're a community built by four passionate tech enthusiasts, led by our caffeine-loving founder, "តែទឹកកក" ("Iced Tea"). We started IT Cafe because we were tired of impersonal workspaces and longed for a place where technology and creativity could flourish together. We poured our coding and design skills into creating a unique space that blends fast Wi-Fi and a dedicated hack space with comfortable seating and delicious, locally-sourced coffee.

Our goal is simple: to foster a welcoming environment where everyone, from coders to creatives, can connect, collaborate, and find inspiration. We host workshops, meetups, and events, all with the aim of building a vibrant community. Whether you're debugging code, brainstorming ideas, or just craving a great cup of coffee, we invite you to plug in, recharge, and join us at IT Cafe. We can't wait to welcome you!
